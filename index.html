ABOUT US

We are a specialized full stack web application development group; made up of user interface & experience designers, product managers, developers and engineering architects.

RESEARCH

We conduct a catered diagnostic through a series of phone and in-person meetings to best understand you and your business. We want to understand from your perspective what the business needs and wants are along with your business structure, limitations, future expectations and brand position.

DISCOVERY

Tailored expectations will then be set based on Site Audit & Competitive Analysis which will be performed by designers and researchers to analyze the business as compared to the overall market. Business related technology development will then be discussed, proposed and finalized for implementation.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Personal Project Managers with an understanding of your business goals will manage your development lifecycle. Not only will you be updated on the development status, but also, consultative insights will be communicated through the entire development lifecycle. The PM will serve as your go-to person and ensure deliverables on the technology aspect are done in a timely fashion. The PM will also consult on and respond to any adjustments that must be made.

UI/UX STRATEGY AND DESIGN

Our UI and UX (User Interface and User Experience) team will be heavily involved in conducting diagnostics to understand the business process and performing Site Audit & Competitive Analysis. This team will take extreme consideration to understand the audiences and the development goal, and transform concepts to visual deliverables before the development begins.

ENGINEERING

A specialized engineering team will be placed to bring the new business concept to life. By using the latest technologies and business tailored services, this team will ensure the product optimizes the opportunities of business development in every aspect.

MAINTENANCE

Optional maintenance services are available to manage your website in the cloud.
